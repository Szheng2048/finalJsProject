Yahtzee

  • Yahtzee is a game where you roll a set of five die and try to get the highest score possible with the dice results
  • You will have the option to mulligan or reroll as many dice as you want once on each roll
  • select the dice you would like to reroll and then press the reroll button
  • The final result will be delegated a single scoring system and you will get points based on the instruction on how to score
  • You can only use each scoring once and must score each result once, if you do not match the conditions the result will be zero
  • Once all 13 of the scoring conditions have a score not counting bonus Yahtzee you must finalize the score and present the grand total
  • The click column is highlighted green for when the scoring procedure has not yet been used, will turn yellow when hovered over and will turn red after click. This allows the user to finalize the results. When all the buttons are red a result will show beside the grand total and will be stored as either the best score or the final score
